Acquired Rarity System (ARS)

Our main purpose in this project is to produce NFTs with Acquired Rarity System (ARS). What we aim here is to remove the current rarity system of NFTs, add new items and rarities to everyone's own NFT, and improve the initial NFTs that can be boring after a time with new and nice-looking ones. That's why we named the Acquired Rarity System the second phase. We wanted to give the right to participate in this phase to people who have a Suzuran Ticket. ARS will work with the help of tasks, and if we need to give examples of these tasks, we would like to explain them as two main assignments. First, earning Steam achievements is important for a gamer, taking this as a rule, a few certain achievements will be added and when these achievements are earned, they will trigger the ARS. Secondly, you will be entitled to participate in the festivals we are hosting and you can receive the ARS’ hidden in some of the special Suzuran events at the festivals. The main purpose of giving these two particular examples is to show that instead of sticking to a single task type, ARS distributes multiple tasks, allowing everyone to concentrate on their own taste while it also helps all participants to work under equal conditions.
