Our Suzuran the biggest internet community in the world. The most important point of our aim is to create a common workspace wherever in the world you are, unlike other communities with weak bonds between their members. Keep in mind that Suzuran has purposes more than a project. There is a suitable role for everybody under Suzuran since it needs you, no matter what you work with. We are working for empowering the projects that are not able to make their ideas real and get the projects released under us.

We are ready to provide every opportunity to create working conditions that will eliminate the importance of where you are in the world. In this project, we want to work and improve together with you. We aim to expand our world map with our new staff and colleagues by starting recruitments in a short time.

Suzuran is a universe created with a great background. Introducing this universe to the whole world and having pleasant moments will be one of our most important aims. For this purpose, we are planning to continue our lore explanations in 2 ways that can be listed as comics and animation series. We want to proceed with them in separate timelines. We want the story told by the comic book and the animation to pass in different time periods and we want people to understand the complete story at the end by merging them.

We aim to produce a roguelike game that most gamers love in the gaming community. Even though it looks the same every time you play, we are hoping to create a work that will not bore you and will make you play it over and over again. We want to eradicate this game genre by taking advantage of the Suzuran's large universe. Via getting inspired by the Dead Cells and the Hades, which are masterpieces of this genre, we aim to present our own creations to you on Steam. The reason we want to present it on Steam is to destroy the influence created by other projects in the game world which is the logic of playing games by earning tokens and to show people what other things can be done.
